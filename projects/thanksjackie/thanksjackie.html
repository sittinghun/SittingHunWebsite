Thanks, Jackie was conceptualized when I saw auto-generated videos on Facebook that let you thank people for their friendship. Using the videos as a creative springboard, I created one to respond to the Rolling Stone sexual assault controversy that swept my university. After the story published in the famous magazine was proven false, this video and I personally received heavy criticism. People found me on social media and personally told me I was wrong to support the alleged sexual assault victim, others called for my expulsion. This video gave me the strength to create controversial content and stay strong in the face of prejudice.
